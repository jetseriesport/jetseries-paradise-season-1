## [EN]

### 🔥 JetSeries Paradise Season 1 — VRS LAN

• **No VRS invites or wildcards** — any team from the Global Region, as defined in Valve’s official regulations, is eligible to sign up.  
• **All stages** — Group Stage and Double-Elimination Playoffs — will take place **offline on LAN** in **Batumi, Georgia**.

---

### 🎮 Main Event (LAN)

• **Dates:** 22 May – 25 May, 2026  
• **Teams:** 4 to 16  
• **Registration:** first-come, first-served  
• **Fee:** $2,000 per team  
• **Group Stage format:** GSL Group, all matches **Bo3**  
• **Playoffs format:** Double-Elimination, all matches **Bo3**  
• **Visa:** Georgia
• **Eligibility:** all team members must be able to attend LAN in Batumi and obtain required visas/passports  
• JetSeries may request proof of travel ability. Failure to provide documentation may lead to removal from the tournament.

---

### ℹ️ Additional Information

• Seeding will follow the official **VRS Rankings** published by Valve in May 
• **HLTV.org**, after our formal application, will determine which stages and matches will count toward VRS. Ranking status is not guaranteed until confirmed  
• **Registration opens:** April 30 at **17:00 CET (20:00 BAT)**  
• JetSeries reserves the right to deny participation to any team or player with an active **VAC ban** or known **integrity violations**  
• Full rulebook will be shared with all participants no later than **two weeks before the event**

---

### 💰 Prize Pool

💰 The entire prize pool will come from the registration fees of participating teams.  
**Team limit — 16.**

⚪️ **Prize Pool:**  
• 4 Teams — $2,500  
• 8 Teams — $5,000  
• 16 Teams — $10,000

---

### 🏆 Distribution

• **1st** — 50%  
• **2nd** — 25%  
• **3rd** — 15%  
• **4th** — 10%

---

### ❗ Important

• Only **one team per organization** may participate  
• JetSeries does **not provide assistance** with visas, travel, accommodation, or meals  
• Teams must confirm participation by **May 15**. Failure to do so will result in the slot being passed to the next registered team  
• A sign-up is considered valid **only after payment is completed**. From the moment you receive the payment link, you have **72 hours** to finalize the payment in order to secure your spot. If payment is not completed in time, the slot will be forwarded to the next team on the list.
