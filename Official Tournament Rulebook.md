# JetSeries Paradise Season 1 - Official Tournament Rulebook

## Event overview
JetSeries Paradise Season 1 is an open **Counter-Strike 2 LAN tournament** organized by JetSeries.  
The tournament is designed as a competitive offline event and is intended to be eligible for **Valve Regional Standings (VRS)**, subject to confirmation by HLTV.org and Valve.  

JetSeries Paradise Season 1 follows Valve’s official rules for ranked tournaments to ensure competitive integrity and a fair tournament structure.  
This rulebook outlines all regulations, format details, and expectations for teams participating in JetSeries Paradise Season 1.
