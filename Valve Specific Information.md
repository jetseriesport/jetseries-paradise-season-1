# Tournament Seeding Information

For seeding purposes, we will be using the **Official Global VRS Ranking for May 2026**.

## Additional Information

- No VRS filter and no VRS invitations. Sign-ups are open to **all teams**.
- Seeding will be based on the official **VRS Rankings** released by Valve in May 2026.
- The event is open to teams from the **Global Region**, as defined in Valve's official rules & regulations.
- The entire tournament, including both the **Qualifying Group Stage** and the **Double Elimination Playoff**, will be played on **LAN at Mercury, Batumi, Georgia**.
- There are **no additional compensations** for teams or players outside of the prize pool.
- Our event aligns with **Valve’s Ranked Tier 2 requirements**. While Tier 2 events may use direct VRS invitations, **Exort will not issue direct invites**.  
  - 16 teams will play the **Group Stage**.  
  - 8 teams will advance from it to the **Main Event**.  
  - Seeding within the Group Stage will be based on **VRS rankings**.

## Tournament Date

**May 22 – May 25, 2026**
